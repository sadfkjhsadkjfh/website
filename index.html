Frequently Asked Questions

What is Loan Against Securities (LAS)?

Loan against securities (LAS) is a facility wherein you can pledge the stocks and mutual funds in your DEMAT account and avail a loan against it. LAS is similar to loan against gold or loan against property.

What is the minimum and maximum amount of loan I can avail?

The minimum amount of loan is Rs 25,000/- and the maximum amount is currently restricted to Rs 10 lakhs. However, if you require loan of more than Rs 10 lakhs, you can request in our app. Our team will get in touch with you and check out the full eligibility.

Why should i choose LAS?

LAS is a secured loan. The process is easy and fully online. Moreover, only outstanding monthly interest is due every month & principal can be repaid any time within 12 months.

How much loan can i get?

The amount of loan depends on the market value of the securities you hold. You can lend upto 50% value of the securities you pledge with us. For example, if the value of the securities is Rs 5,00,000 then a loan up to Rs 2,50,000 can be disbursed.

Can I pre-close my loan early? Are there any charges?

Yes, you can pre-close and pre-pay your loan amount at any time. For example, if you repay your loan amount within 3 months, you will only have to pay interest on your loan for 3 months and nothing more. There are no charges with respect to prepayment or closing your loan early.
